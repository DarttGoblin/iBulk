The 5 Days Bro Split is structured into five sessions, each focusing on specific muscle groups to ensure a well-rounded and effective workout routine.

Session 1: Chest and Triceps (4 Supersets):

Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretches.
Chest Superset 1: Bench Press (4 sets x 10 reps) paired with Push-ups (4 sets x 15 reps).
Chest Superset 2: Incline Dumbbell Press (3 sets x 12 reps) paired with Chest Flyes (3 sets x 15 reps).
Triceps Superset: Tricep Dips (3 sets x 12 reps) paired with Overhead Tricep Extension (3 sets x 15 reps).

Session 2: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves):

Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio and leg stretches.
Quads: Barbell Back Squats (4 sets x 12 reps).
Hamstrings: Romanian Deadlifts (3 sets x 10 reps).
Calves: Standing Calf Raises (3 sets x 15 reps).

Session 3: Arms (Biceps, Triceps, Forearms):

Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio and arm stretches.
Biceps: Barbell Curl (4 sets x 12 reps).
Triceps: Skull Crushers (3 sets x 10 reps).
Forearms: Farmer's Walk (3 sets x 1 minute).

Session 4: Shoulders (3 Supersets):

Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio and shoulder stretches.
Superset 1: Seated Dumbbell Press (4 sets x 10 reps) paired with Lateral Raises (4 sets x 15 reps).
Superset 2: Front Plate Raise (3 sets x 12 reps) paired with Upright Rows (3 sets x 12 reps).

Session 5: Back and Biceps (5 Supersets):

Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio and back stretches.
Back Superset 1: Bent Over Rows (4 sets x 10 reps) paired with Lat Pulldowns (4 sets x 12 reps).
Back Superset 2: Face Pulls (3 sets x 15 reps) paired with Hyperextensions (3 sets x 12 reps).
Biceps Superset: Hammer Curls (3 sets x 15 reps) paired with Concentration Curls (3 sets x 12 reps).

Notes:

Finish each session with 10 minutes of static stretching focusing on the muscle groups worked.
Adjust weights and repetitions based on individual fitness levels.
Stay hydrated and maintain proper form throughout each exercise.
Listen to your body, and if you have any health concerns, consult with a fitness professional or healthcare provider.
